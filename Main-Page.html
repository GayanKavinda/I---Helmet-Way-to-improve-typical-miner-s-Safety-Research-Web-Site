OUR RESEARCH DOMAIN

For a long time, people have been digging the earth for various minerals. Mining in the economies of developing countries such as India, Argentina, Armenia, and Sri Lanka is a major source of foreign exchange. These mine related accidents kill a large number of people every year and the main theme of our project is to ensure the safety of the lives of the workers working in the mines. The main challenge for all miners in the world today is the safety of life. Mining is one of the most dangerous activities in the world. The highest occupational mortality rate occurs in the mining industry. The main objective of this project is to reduce the risk of mining and increase efficiency. The safety of the miners in the mining industry is very important. Common causes of occupational death are mine crashes, explosions, toxic gases and electric shocks. Currently, miners usually wear only a helmet with a light for visibility. Therefore, their safety is very low. Our proposed system consists of a smart and automated i-Helmet finding feature that can help miners to improve safety.

 MAIN COMPONENTS

  •  ​To explore and identify environmental condition of the mining site with data visualization
  •  ​ To explore and identify mining site physical structure its safety and hazards
  •  ​To explore and identify the environmental air quality and notification to the end user
  •   ​To explore, automate, predicted, and notify health condition of the mining worker

Objectives of the research component:

  • Detects accidents in the mine by the camera module and notifies the Chief Control Center. Identify the level of harmful dust in the field and identify fires in the mine and inform the Chief Control Center.

Objectives of the research component:

  • Detect after a heavy object has fallen on the workers' heads in the mine, identify cracks in the walls of the mine and the moisture inside the mine, and notify the main control center.

Objectives of the research component:

  • Detects harmful gases in the mine and identifies the miner's alcohol level and notifies the main control center.

Objectives of the research component:

  • Detects the miner's oxygen level and temperature and sends it to the main control center. If there is a patient then seek medical advice through the telemedicine app.
